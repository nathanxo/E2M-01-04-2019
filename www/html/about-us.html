ABOUT US

Headquartered in West Midlands, "Express 2 Man", a '2 man white-glove courier' provides an incomparable offering of services including next-day and nominated day delivery seven days a week, which is what sets us apart from competitors. Goods carried consist of home and office furniture and furnishings, white goods, brown goods, kitchen and bedrooms, garden and fitness equipment. With customer care at the very heart of its business, Express 2 Man prides itself on ensuring a high-calibre service for customers and accommadate a complete end-to-end solution to meet the growing needs of the home delivery marketplace. THE TECHNOLOGY WE USE FOR YOU
We use a cloud-based software that consists of intelligent and route optimisation which allows our customers to swiftly process orders through our online booking form. This enables our customers to get electronic Proof of Delivery (ePODs) and allows them to track their order online. This technology also allows us and our customers to track drivers in real time so we make sure that your order is going to the right place.
